French Toast

Description

The best french toast of your life! Works with many types of bread - white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian or French! Served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • bread of your choice (6 slices)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Directions:

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and salt together in a shallow bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat
  4. Dunk bread into egg mixture, soaking both sides
  5. Transfer bread to skillet and cook until golden (3 or 4 minutes on each side)
  6. Serve hot with butter and maple syrup as desired
